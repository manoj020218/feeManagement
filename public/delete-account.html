Delete Account

Permanently erase your FeeFlow account and all associated authentication data.

⚠️ This action is irreversible.
What gets deleted:
  • ✓ Your phone number, name, and hashed PIN from our authentication server.
  • ✓ Any Google Sign‑In link (if used).
  • ✗ Your local fee data (members, receipts, payments) remains on your device – you must manually clear app data or uninstall.
  • ✗ Google Drive backups (if any) are NOT automatically deleted. You must delete them from your own Google Drive.
  • ✗ VPS sync data (if self-hosted) remains on your VPS.

To delete your account, open the FeeFlow app → Settings → Delete Account. Or send a request from your registered email to manoj020218@gmail.com with subject "Delete My FeeFlow Account". We will process within 7 business days.
