Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2026

Jenix ("we", "us", or "our") operates the FeeFlow mobile app and web application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use FeeFlow.

1. What Data We Collect (Minimal)

We collect only the absolute minimum required to authenticate you:

  • Phone number – used as your unique identifier and for optional WhatsApp reminders.
  • Name – to personalise your account.
  • Hashed PIN – for secure login (we never store your plain-text PIN).
  • Google account email (if you choose Google Sign‑In) – only for authentication.

We do NOT collect, store, or ever have access to: your members' names, phone numbers, fee records, payment amounts, receipts, institution names, or any financial transaction data. All such data lives exclusively on your device, your Google Drive backup (your personal account), or your own VPS if you self-host.

2. Where Your Data Lives

All fee‑related data (members, payments, receipts, institution settings) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage (web app) or Android's internal storage (mobile app). If you enable Google Drive backup, data is encrypted and sent directly to your personal Google Drive – we have no access. If you configure your own VPS sync, data goes to your server.

3. WhatsApp & SMS Reminders

If you (as admin) enable reminders, FeeFlow may trigger WhatsApp messages or SMS to your members via your device's default messaging or WhatsApp integration. We do not store or log these messages.

4. Third-Party Services

FeeFlow uses Google Sign-In (OAuth) and Google Drive API (with your explicit permission). These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party analytics or trackers are embedded.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your entire account and all associated data at any time from the "Delete Account" page. This permanently removes your phone number, name, and hashed PIN from our authentication server. Your local data (on device) must be cleared separately via app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

FeeFlow is not directed at children under 13. However, institutions may store records of minors – that data remains under the institution's control and is never transmitted to our servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified via the app or website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at manoj020218@gmail.com or visit the Contact page.
