About FeeFlow

FeeFlow is a privacy-first, offline-capable fee management system built for schools, gyms, coaching centers, libraries, housing societies, and every institution that needs to collect recurring fees.

Our Mission

We believe financial data belongs to you. Most fee apps store your members' names, phone numbers, and payment records on their servers — then charge you to access your own data. FeeFlow flips the model: all fee records, member lists, and receipts stay on your device or your private cloud (Google Drive / your own VPS). Our servers only store your name, phone, and a hashed PIN for login — never any financial transaction.

Why We Built FeeFlow

Running a small academy or a large school shouldn't require expensive software or exposing your members' privacy. We built FeeFlow to be free, transparent, and completely under your control. With automated WhatsApp reminders, instant PDF receipts, and a member dashboard that shows pending dues in real time, FeeFlow saves hours of manual follow-up every month.

Who We Serve

School administrators, gym owners, library managers, coaching centre coordinators, housing society treasurers, hostel wardens, music academy directors, sports club secretaries, salon/spa owners, and skill training institutes.
